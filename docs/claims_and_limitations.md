# Claims and Limitations

## Supported Claims

- Source-native structural noised-gold negatives and behavior negatives move
  different evaluation axes under the fixed DPO budget.
- Tool-use DPO should be reported as a Pareto trade-off across intended gains,
  guardrail regressions, and checkpoint choice.
- Early checkpoints can be preferable reporting points when guardrail metrics are
  considered.
- Matched diagnostics characterize source-quality risks, but do not authorize
  downstream matched-DPO claims in this release.

## Unsupported Claims

- The results do not prove intrinsic source superiority.
- The results do not show universal clean-filtering superiority.
- The results do not establish equal-token causal control.
- The matched diagnostic set does not support reporting matched mini-DPO results.
